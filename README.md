# Meta Ad Performance Dashboard | Power BI

A Power BI dashboard analyzing Meta (Facebook) ad campaign performance across key metrics 
like impressions, clicks, conversions, and budget — with audience, geographic, and time-based insights.

---

## Features

- **KPI Cards** — Top-level metrics for quick performance overview
- **Audience Analysis** — Engagement breakdown by Gender and Age Group
- **Geographic Map** — Country-wise engagement distribution
- **Ad Type Comparison** — CTR, Conversion Rate, and Engagement Rate for Carousel, Stories, Image, and Video
- **Time Trends** — Weekly and Hourly engagement trends
- **Calendar Filter** — Month-level drill-down analysis
- **Dynamic Filters** — Filter by Campaign Name, Target Interests, and Metric type

---

## Key Insights

1. **CTR of 11.79%** is well above industry average — ad creatives and targeting were effective
2. **Purchase rate is only 0.60%** — strong awareness but conversion funnel needs optimization
3. **Females (41%)** and the **18–30 age group** drive the majority of engagements
4. **Video and Stories** ads outperform Carousel and Image in CTR and conversion
5. **India and Brazil** lead in engagement volume — Germany and UK represent higher-value markets
6. Engagement peaks during **afternoon and evening hours** — best time to schedule ads
